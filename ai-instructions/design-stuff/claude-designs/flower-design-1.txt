Each flower is anchored so the center of the bloom sits at the `translate(...)` coordinates, with the stem extending downward. If you want to drop them into a scene at a specific location, just change that `translate` value. The cherry blossom, rose, and poppy use slightly different viewBox heights because their blooms sit more compactly on their stems than the tall daisy, tulip, and sunflower.
