# Cookie Allergy Highlights In Receipt Card

We need allergy callouts on the cookie info receipt/card, with reddish styling that can be controlled by admins in Payload.

Scope:

- Add an admin field for allergy warnings or allergy highlight text on cookie/product records.
- Render that content on the cookie info receipt/card with a clearly distinct reddish treatment.
- Keep the field optional so products without allergy notes do not show an empty section.

Notes:

- The admin label should be business-friendly, not overly technical.
- Design should read as a warning/caution without looking like an error state.
